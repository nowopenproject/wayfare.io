Gatherings

Our Costa Mesa HQ location boasts in over 3,300 sq/ft of open event space outfitted with everything you need to make any gathering a unique and memorable occassion.

This space hosts frequent gatherings and workshops on the weekends and on weekday evenings (after 5pm). So whether you'd like to try your hand in calligraphy or host your next non-profit fundraiser with us, we're glad you're here and we hope you join us soon!

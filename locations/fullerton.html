About this Space

Located in the heart of downtown, Wayfare Fullerton occupies the ground floor of the historic Chapman Building. This space features private offices on the upstairs mezzanine, and coworking, lounges, meeting rooms, and a coffee bar. Buzzing with the life of downtown, Wayfare Fullerton is walking distance from endless amounts of food and retail. Less than an hour from LAX, this location is the ideal space for client meetings, conferences, fundraisers, and much more.

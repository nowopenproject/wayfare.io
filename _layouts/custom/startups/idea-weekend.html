Overview

Idea Weekend is designed to help you begin bringing to life that world-changing idea that you are passionate about. Whether you are a founder with a brand new idea or you are piloting a new product within your existing organization, Pilot Weekend is designed specifically for you.

Idea Weekend kick-starts your plan and helps you create the roadmap for your idea to become a reality alongside a community of other dreamers, doers, and adventurers for the common good.Idea Weekend combines insight from startup experts with hands-on application in a workshop setting. You will leave with a roadmap of action steps to pilot your idea and create a minimum viable product or service.

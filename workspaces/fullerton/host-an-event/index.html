Host an Event at Wayfare

Fullerton’s clean, upscale design make it perfectly suited for a wide variety of tastes and styles, where you are free to add your own personal touches and accomplish he exact feel you desire. The open layout, high industrial ceilings, vaulted skylights, and dazzling sophisticated chandeliers create the perfect canvas and spotlight for your unique occasion.

Wayfare is ideally suited for workshops, business events & non-profit gatherings; but we would love to learn about whatever your occasion may be! Choose your event category below for more information & to submit your inquiry.

Social Enterprise & Non-Profit Gatherings

We love being able to partner with non-profits and social enterprises to bring their mission to life in the form of fundraiser events, trainings, meetings, retreats and more!

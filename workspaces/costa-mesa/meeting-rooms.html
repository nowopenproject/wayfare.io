Meeting Rooms

Wayfare Costa Mesa offers a professional & design-savvy meeting environment like no other amongst a dynamic, supportive & like-minded community.

You will be greeted by our in-house coffee shop, Neat Coffee, where you can start your meeting with a honey cinnamon latte or a shot of Stumptown espresso.

Large Meeting Room

$80/hour

  • Seats up to 8 people
  • 80" TV equipped with Apple TV/Airplay for presentations and video
  • Two large whiteboards (one is magnetic!) + dry erase markers
  • Conference table with 6 built-in power outlets and 5 USB outlets
  • Outlets around the room
  • Lamps for ambient lighting

Small Meeting Room

$50/hour

  • Seats up to 4 people
  • 50" TV with AppleTV/Airplay for presentations and video
  • Large 8'x4' whiteboard + dry-erase markers
  • Conference table with 3 built-in power outlets and 2 USB outlets
  • Outlets around the room
  • Lamps for ambient lighting

Semi-Private Alcoves

$30/hour

  • Seat up to 4 people
  • 70" TV with AppleTV/Airplay for presentations and video
  • Large 5'x7' whiteboard + dry-erase markers
  • Chalk wall + chalk for the ultimate brainstorming experience
  • Configurable rooms with standard rolling desks and chairs
  • 3 walls and a curtain that can be drawn across the opening
  • Outlets around the room
  • Lamps for ambient lighting

* Specialty coffee drinks are also available for additional purchase at our in-house coffee bar.

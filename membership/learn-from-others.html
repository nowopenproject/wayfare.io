Learn from Others

Forum! Get a chance to learn from social good pioneers in real time. Forum is a monthly lunch event hosted at Wayfare locations, and all members are invited to join via live-cast or in person.

Advisor hours! Meet with experts in specific fields who have subject matter expertise to help you with your work-specific questions and challenges.

Receive member-only discounts to attend a variety of workshops such as Intro to Modern Calligraphy, Social Media, Healthy Body, Yoga for Work, Intro to Graphic Design, and many more. Workshops are invaluable great resources for personal and professional growth, as well as a great date-night outlet.
